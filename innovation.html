Innovation – AI-Based Skin Disease Detection System

Rita Watiri

This innovation project focuses on an Artificial Intelligence (AI)–based system for detecting common skin diseases such as acne, eczema, ringworm, and melanoma. The system uses deep learning techniques to analyze skin images and provide preliminary diagnoses, improving early detection, accessibility, and healthcare outcomes.

Download Project Presentation (PPT)

The project was developed using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to classify skin conditions from images uploaded by users. It demonstrates the practical application of AI in healthcare technology, particularly in regions where access to dermatologists is limited. The system aims to empower users with early detection tools, while supporting healthcare professionals in accurate and timely diagnoses.
