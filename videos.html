Video 1: Agikuyu clan and the Nine Daughters

English Abstract: The story of the Nine Daughters of the Kikuyu, descendants of Gĩkũyũ and Mumbi, is a central part of Kikuyu cultural heritage, illustrating the origins of the nine clans that make up the community. This narrative highlights the social, spiritual, and moral values of Kikuyu society and reflects the roles and expectations assigned to women and men. Examining this story through the lens of gender awareness demonstrates the importance of recognizing women’s contributions in cultural preservation and community development. This essay explores how the Nine Daughters myth can be used to promote gender sensitivity while maintaining the richness of African cultural identity.

Kikuyu Abstract: Hadithi ya Wasichana Tisa wa Kikuyu, wajukuu wa Gĩkũyũ na Mumbi, ni sehemu muhimu ya urithi wa kitamaduni wa Kikuyu, ikionyesha asili ya makabila tisa yanayounda jamii ya Kikuyu. Hadithi hii inaonyesha maadili ya kijamii, kiroho, na maadili ya jamii ya Kikuyu na inaakisi nafasi na matarajio yaliyopewa wanawake na wanaume. Kuchunguza hadithi hii kwa mtazamo wa uelewa wa jinsia kunaonyesha umuhimu wa kutambua mchango wa wanawake katika kuhifadhi utamaduni na maendeleo ya jamii. Makala hii inachunguza jinsi hadithi ya Wasichana Tisa inaweza kutumika kukuza uelewa wa jinsia huku ikihifadhi utajiri wa utambulisho wa kitamaduni wa Kiafrika.

Ũthuthuria wa rũgano rũrũ kũgerera gĩthimi kĩa ũiganĩru wa kĩmĩrĩra (gender understanding) nĩũguũragia bata wa kũhe atumia gĩtĩyo kĩrĩa kĩonagĩria itemi rĩao harĩ kũhũndĩra ũndũire na gũteithia gũkũra kwa jamanda. English Abstract: This presentation explores the critical challenge of youth unemployment in modern society, particularly within the African context. While formal job markets shrink, there remains a vast, untapped potential in cultural industries and community-based social enterprises. Drawing inspiration from the traditional Kikuyu social structure—specifically the "Nine Daughters" as a symbol of decentralized productivity and communal support—this study proposes a "Heritage-Driven Entrepreneurship" model. The solution focuses on integrating indigenous knowledge with modern technology to create sustainable livelihoods. By re-evaluating gender roles and communal responsibility, the presentation argues that the solution to unemployment lies not just in industrialization, but in reclaiming the economic agency inherent in our cultural identity

Kikuyu Abstract: Mũtaratara ũyũ nĩũratuĩria thĩna mũnene wa wagi wa mawĩra harĩ agathĩka (youth) thĩinĩ wa itũũra rĩa rĩu, na mũno mũno gũkũ Abĩrika. O rĩrĩ thoko cia mawĩra ma mbebeta irathiĩ ikĩnyihaga, nĩ kũrĩ na ũhoti mũnene thĩinĩ wa maũndũ ma ũndũire na biacara cia kĩ-itũũra ĩrĩa ĩtahũthĩrĩtwo. Tũkĩhũthĩra kĩonereria kĩa mũbangĩre wa tene wa Agĩkũyũ—mũno mũno airĩtu kenda a Gĩkũyũ na Mũmbi ta kĩonereria kĩa ũtonga na kũteithania—ũthuthuria ũyũ ũraregenda njĩra ĩrĩa ĩtagwo "Biacara cia Kĩ-ũndũire." Kĩhonia kĩrĩa kĩraheanwo nĩ kũnyitanĩria ũmenyo wa tene na mĩtugo ya kĩ-teknonoji nĩguo kũũmbũra mawĩra ma gũtũũra. Kũgerera gũtuĩria rĩngĩ itemi rĩa atumia na arũme hamwe na wĩra wa itũũra, mũtaratara ũyũ ũronania atĩ kĩhonia kĩa wagi wa mawĩra tĩ gũaka mabuloti ma iganda tu, no nĩ kũcookerera ũhoti wa kĩ-ũtonga ũrĩa ũrĩ thĩinĩ wa ũndũire witũ.

Swahili Abstract: Wasilisho hili linachunguza changamoto kubwa ya ukosefu wa ajira kwa vijana katika jamii ya sasa, hasa katika mazingira ya Kiafrika. Wakati soko la ajira rasmi likizidi kupungua, kuna uwezo mkubwa usiotumiwa katika tasnia za kitamaduni na biashara za kijamii. Kwa kupata msukumo kutoka kwa muundo wa kijamii wa kitamaduni wa Gĩkũyũ—hususan "Wasichana Tisa" kama ishara ya uzalishaji uliogatuliwa na usaidizi wa kijamii—utafiti huu unapendekeza mfano wa "Ujasiriamali Unaotokana na Urithi." Suluhisho linajikita katika kuunganisha maarifa asilia na teknolojia ya kisasa ili kutengeneza fursa za kimaisha endelevu. Kwa kutathmini upya nafasi za kijinsia na majukumu ya jamii, wasilisho hili linahoji kuwa suluhu la ukosefu wa ajira halipo tu katika viwanda, bali katika kurejesha uwezo wa kiuchumi uliopo ndani ya utambulisho wetu wa kitamaduni.

English Abstract: Skin diseases remain one of the most common global health burdens, yet diagnostic accuracy is often hampered by limited access to specialists and the subjective nature of visual exams. This innovation focuses on an AI-powered mobile application that utilizes Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to identify over 20 types of skin conditions from smartphone images. Key innovations include Explainable AI (XAI), which provides heatmaps to show clinicians which part of the image triggered a diagnosis, and the use of Synthetic Image Datasets to improve accuracy for underrepresented skin of color (melanin-rich skin). By integrating patient history with image analysis, the system provides a high-accuracy decision-support tool for rural practitioners, bridging the gap between urban specialists and remote patients.

Kikuyu Abstract: Mĩrimũ ya ngothi nĩ ĩmwe ya mathĩna manene ma ũgima wa mwĩrĩ thĩinĩ wa thĩ, no maita maingĩ kũmenya mĩrimũ ĩyo nĩ gũritũhanga nĩ ũndũ wa kwaga mabingĩrĩrie na ũhoti wa gũthuthuria na maitho tu. Ũgarũrũku ũyũ ũraregenda ruo rwa thĩmũ (mobile app) rũhũthĩrĩte Akĩlĩ-Mnemba (AI) nĩguo kũmenya mĩrimũ mĩhembe makĩria ma mĩhĩrĩga mĩrongo ĩĩrĩ kũgerera mbica cia thĩmũ. Maũndũ njerũ marĩa marahũthĩrwo nĩ hamwe na AI ĩrĩa ĩrataũra (Explainable AI), ĩrĩa ĩtuagĩria ndagĩtarĩ kĩrĩa kĩatũma ũthuthuria ũheanwo, na gũthaka na mbica cia kĩ-mĩatĩ (synthetic images) nĩguo kũigana ũthuthuria harĩ andũ arĩa marĩ na ngoro njirũ (melanin-rich skin). Kũgerera kũnyitanĩria ũhoro wa mũrwarũ na mbica cia mwĩrĩ, mũtaratara ũyũ nĩũrateithĩrĩria madagĩtarĩ ma itũũra kũheana ũtumanĩria mwega, na kũnyitanĩria mwanya ũrĩa ũrĩ gatagatĩ ka ndagĩtarĩ nene cia taũni na andũ arĩa marĩ kũraya.
