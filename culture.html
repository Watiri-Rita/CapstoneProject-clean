Culture – Agikuyu Culture: Kirira

Rita Watiri

This project explores the Agikuyu culture with a focus on Kirira, highlighting its customs, rituals, and significance within the community. The presentation demonstrates how understanding traditional practices can inform modern professional disciplines, especially in technology and education, by respecting cultural heritage while innovating responsibly.

Download Culture Presentation (PPT)

The presentation delves into traditional Kirira ceremonies, beliefs, and practices, emphasizing their cultural and social relevance. By documenting and analyzing these customs, the project contributes to preserving cultural heritage and educating others on the role of Agikuyu traditions in contemporary society. This understanding is especially important for projects and innovations that aim to be socially and culturally aware.
