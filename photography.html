Photography – Access to Education in Africa

Rita Watiri

This photographic essay highlights one of the most pressing issues facing the African community today: access to education. Through photography, we explore the challenges that prevent children and young adults from attaining quality education, including long distances to schools, lack of resources, and socioeconomic barriers.

Download Full Photographic Essay (PDF)

The essay captures real-life scenarios across different regions, showing the struggles and resilience of students and educators. It emphasizes the importance of investing in educational infrastructure, resources, and community support. By raising awareness through visual storytelling, this project aims to inspire action towards better educational opportunities for all African children.
